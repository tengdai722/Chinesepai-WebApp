你好 :)

Bryan from Taiwan! I am a Chinese native speaker as well as a government certified Chinese teacher. In our lesson, we use Chinese / English as the teaching medium depending on your proficiency level. If you don't speak any of the languages above, don't worry, there are other ways to communicate and make you learn with ease :)

Any level is a good level because we are going to find out which methods best suits your learning style and needs. ( I'm learning foreign languages too and I know the struggles...)

Quick Facts:

1. The 4 skill training (speaking, listening, reading, writing) are subject to the student's need. You may feel free to choose the area you'd like to work on. My suggestion would be starting from listening and speaking, then gradually moving to reading and writing (the 4 skills compliment each other as you learn)

2. For absolute beginners, the lesson will help you familiarize with Pinyin / MPS and tones. Wouldn't it be great to speak with clear, understandable pronunciation?

3. For students with a certain level, the speaking and listening training is provided to help you develop the ability to converse.

4. For students who are interested in traditional Chinese characters or Taiwanese culture, you have come to the right place :)

5. For students with special learning request, feel free to leave me a message for customized lessons.

--- Friendly Reminders ---

@ Please try to book at least 2-3 days ahead, many thanks~

@ For first-time students, please kindly let me know:

1) How you would describe your Chinese level (e.g. HSK 1 or how well you speak/listen)

2) Which area you might want to work on

3) If you have your own textbook or you're open to different materials

@ The trial lesson is 30 min.

@ If your availability doesn't match the current timetable, it can be discussed.
